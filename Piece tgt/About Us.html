About Us

Who are 'wee'?

'Wee' are a family of crochet enthusiasts that started out the ChubbsCrochet in January 2021. Starting from the right, we have Jing Hui (oldest sister), Ting Hui (second sister) and lastly Yan Kai (youngest brother).



Wee Jing Hui


Roles: Main creator of ChubbsCrochet and the main crochetor of the products


Jinghui was inspired by a local crochet artist, Kelly Limerick. In 2018, she came across a video of Kelly Limerick, which showcased the artist crochet journey and some artwork. Intrigued by Kelly Limerick's crochet artwork, Jinghui did more findings on crochet and it has helped her widen her perspective on crochet. Jinghui took up crochet in March 2020, self taught crochet through online free patterns and video tutorials.



Wee Ting Hui


Roles: Handle sales and marketing of products, handle social media platforms


Tinghui has an interest in arts and crafts, especially DIY crafts. She was roped in by her sister to help out ChubbsCrochet, by handling the admin matters, such as managing the sales of products and social media platform. She occassionally helped out with crochet products, by crocheting and giving some ideas design, when she is more free.



Wee Yan Kai


Roles: Designer of amigurumi and maintenance of website


Yankai had the knowledge of web designing and hence he was tasked to maintain the website, updating the necessary information. In addition, he was assigned to be the main designer for the amigurumi, as he has a talent for designing.

What do 'wee' do?

ChubbsCrochet was created to serve as a platform for other crochet lovers to come together and share tips and information on crochet. It is also a website to increase the exposure about crochet.
